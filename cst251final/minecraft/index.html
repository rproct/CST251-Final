About the game.

This game is the best selling video game of all time with over 180 million copies sold across all platforms. Minecraft has a blocky, 3D look to it where you can discover, build, explore, and get items. This game allows you to: extract materials, craft tools, build structures, fight computer generated people, as well as being able to communicate with other real love players in the game. There are different modes as well, such as survival mode and creative mode. This game has been said to be one of the greatest and most influential video games of all time.
