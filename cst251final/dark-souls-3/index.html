Why you should buy: Dark Souls 3

Dark Souls 3 is the perfect game to challenge yourself with during this quarantine. Dark Souls is renowned for its difficulty, but with that difficulty comes immense satisfaction when progressing through the game. Dark Souls is so hard that dying is literally one of the core mechanics in the game. Don't be offput, though - the game is challenging, but it is not unfair. With some practice and some knowledge, you too can be putting your enemies in their places.

My favorite way to play.

I personally love going for a strength, two-handed weapon build. I focus on doing as much damage as possible per hit and staggering my opponents with my unwieldy weapons. Being able to one or two shot weak enemies is quite satisfying to me. The only problem with using slow weapons is that you must know the rhythm of combat well so you aren't left vulnerable to counterattacks.
